Good to know

The questions clinics ask first.

Is the teardown really free?

Yes. You tell us how enquiries reach you now; we send back a short, specific teardown of where bookings are slipping. If it's useful, we talk about building it. If not, you keep the teardown.

What happens to our patients' data?

Aesthetic enquiries are health data, so we treat them that way. Anything with personal detail is processed on a private model — never a public AI tool that could train on it. We sign a Data Processing Agreement and EU Standard Contractual Clauses before anything goes live.

Do we have to install or learn anything?

No new app for your team. It runs on the channels you already use — WhatsApp, your website form, Instagram, your calendar — and hands anything sensitive straight to a human on your rules.

Where are you based?

We're a remote AI systems studio based in South Africa, working with clinics across the UK and Ireland. We operate as a GDPR-compliant data processor, with the full data agreement pack in place before go-live.

Won't it sound robotic to our patients?

It's tuned to your tone and your treatments, gives price ranges rather than hard quotes, and books a consultation — exactly how your best front-of-house would handle it. You approve the wording before it ever talks to a patient.

What if it's wrong about something?

It escalates. Clinical questions, complaints, or anything outside its rules get flagged to a human. You stay in control; it just stops the easy bookings from slipping away at 9pm.
